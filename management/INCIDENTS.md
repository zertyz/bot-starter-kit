Incidents record production, staging, data, security, or release failures that teach the project something operationally useful.

## Incident Entry Format

### INCIDENT-0001 -- `<short title>`

Status:
`Open`, `Mitigated`, or `Closed`

Detected:
`YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM timezone`

Affected environment:
`Production`, `Staging`, `Development`, or `Local`

Impact:
`<user, data, security, availability, or delivery impact>`

Timeline:
1. `YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM` -- `<event>`

Root cause:
`<cause after investigation>`

Mitigation:
`<what stopped or reduced the impact>`

Follow-up work:
1. `<work item id>` -- `<summary>`

Requirement or decision changes:
1. `<requirement id or decision id>` -- `<change needed>`
